Islamic Inheritance Law (Faraid) Basics

Faraid is the Islamic system of inheritance distribution prescribed in the Quran. Fixed shares are assigned to specific heirs (spouse, parents, children), with the remainder going to residuary heirs.

This calculator handles the most common family structures. For complex cases involving grandchildren, siblings, half-siblings, or multiple marriages, consult a qualified Islamic scholar or Faraid specialist.
