Understanding Sukuk Returns

Sukuk are Islamic financial certificates similar to bonds but structured to comply with Sharia law. Instead of earning interest, Sukuk holders receive a share of profit generated by the underlying asset.

The yield to maturity accounts for both the periodic profit distributions and any capital gain or loss at maturity (difference between purchase price and face value).
